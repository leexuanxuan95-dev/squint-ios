Frequently asked

Why does Squint need the camera?

Only to measure how far your face is from the screen, so the test letters are the right size. The image is never saved or sent — see our Privacy Policy.

My phone says distance is unavailable.

Precise distance needs a TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later). On older devices, Squint skips the distance-based Snellen check and still offers the astigmatism, contrast, near-vision, and 20-20-20 features.

Is Squint a medical test?

No. Squint is a wellness self-check that compares results to your own baseline. It does not diagnose anything. For any concern, see an optometrist.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On your iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Squint → Cancel.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open any paywall in Squint and tap "Restore Purchases."
